Max Temperature

As expected, the weather becomes slightly warmer as one approaches the equator (0 degree latitude). More interestingly, however, is the fact that the southern hemispheretends to be warmer this time of year than the northern hemisphere. This may be due to the tilt of the earth at the time of the year that the data was gathered.
